SOLARPUNK: What in the world?

Solarpunk is an idealized vision that focuses on renewable energy, harmonious living with nature and the like. This is also associated with anti-capitalism behavior and a strong sense of community among the people.

As shown in the photos, in the concept of Solarpunk, nature is highly integrated in society, with greens surrounding almost every inch of people's lives! This may seem like an ideal concept for the world; however, it all seems too good to be true. Now, we ask: Is this really an ideal state of living? How will society function? What are the cons? Is this even attainable?
